Note: Liberal policy scores are calculated based on 135 policies classified as either liberal or conservative. A liberal policy is defined as expanding state power for economic regulation and redistribution or for protecting marginalized groups, or restricting state power for punishing deviant social behavior; a conservative policy is defined as the opposite. For example, legalization of medical marijuana is categorized as a liberal policy, and a gay marriage ban is categorized as a conservative policy. In the map, states that shifted towards more liberal policies are shaded blue. States that shifted towards more conservative policies are shaded green.
